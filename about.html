About I Have An Idea

I HAVE AN IDEA is a business incubator based in the sub saharan African region of Nigeria. Amassing a vast timeline of networks, and built from the foundation of inspiration ourselves, we understand the journey of the startup company, and thus our offering includes the right structural framework, the right partnerships, and of course, the financial accessibility, to support your idea; charting out the optimal road map for its business, and getting it where you want it to be. And although our competences seem to be targetted to the beginning stages of business, our invitation for partnership is in fact open to mid tier, and long standing organizations as well. Thus fostering growth from any level that your business is on. We believe that I HAVE AN IDEA is the right choice for you, in the angel investment industry.



Our Mission:

Our mission is simple yet profound: to discover and support groundbreaking ideas that have the potential to make a significant impact. We are dedicated to fostering innovation and providing entrepreneurs with the tools they need to succeed.

Our Vision

We envision a world where creativity thrives, and innovative ideas have the opportunity to reach their full potential. By offering funding and strategic marketing, we aim to be the catalyst for the next wave of successful businesses.
